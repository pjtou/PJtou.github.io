This site presents Hindu (or Vedic) Astrology, various articles, links, and the Winvega software.  The astrology research here is based on the classic "Brihat Parasara Hora Sastra" and Upadesa Sutra by Jaimini.

There are many books and classics down the ages about Jyotish astrology, but only Brihat Parasara (short BPHS) has been repeteadly described as coming from a Rishi (Sage).   Most commentators and researchers describe it as the closest thing to the original source of ancient Jyotish as it was meant to be taught.   As it was actually revealled to humans from a divine source, if we are to believe the text.   It is also the most complete and inclusive astrology textbook anywhere.  In it is found the mathematical astrology, all the main yogas, many period systems (dasa), signs, planets and various Ascendant (Lagna) techniques and delineations. There isnt any astrology as complete anywhere, east or west.
But in truth while most astrologers of Jyotish in India and the world present themselves as practicing the Parasara method, few actually apply themselves to what Parasara actually taught.   Hence this site.
